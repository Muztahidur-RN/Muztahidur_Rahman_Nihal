About Me

I was born on 14 March 2008 and I am currently a Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) candidate for 2026 from a science background. I completed my PSC examination in 2018 with GPA 5, and later passed my SSC examination in 2024 with a GPA of 4.50. I am now continuing my academic journey with the goal of achieving GPA 5 in HSC.


My interest in technology grew stronger when I got my own computer during Class 9. From that point, I started developing my computer skills alongside my academic studies. I explored the basics of editing, design, and programming, which gradually led me toward web development and modern technologies.


In 2025, I was selected to participate in the National Programming Contest, held at the Institute of Information Technology (IIT), University of Dhaka. This experience became a turning point for me—it motivated me greatly and strengthened my determination to continue learning and improving my coding skills.


Alongside my passion for technology, I have a strong sense of responsibility toward my country. In the future, I aim to serve the Bangladesh Air Force as a professional fighter plane pilot, beginning my career through national service. Beyond that, I also dream of building a large-scale super shop where people can find all their daily necessities in one place. I call this idea BlinkHubMall, which reflects my vision of convenience, accessibility, and service.


Thank you for taking the time to learn about my journey. I hope to grow into someone who can contribute positively to my country, humanity, and the world.
